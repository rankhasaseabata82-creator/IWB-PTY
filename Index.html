Fighting e-Waste, Forging a Sustainable Future

Join us in our mission to reduce electronic waste and create a greener planet for generations to come.

Our Mission Against e-Waste

The Problem

Electronic waste is the fastest-growing waste stream in the world, with over 50 million metric tons generated annually. This e-waste contains hazardous materials that can leach into soil and water, posing serious environmental and health risks.

Only 20% of e-waste is formally recycled, with the rest often ending up in landfills or being improperly processed in developing countries, exposing workers to dangerous substances.

Our Solution

IWB PTY LTD is committed to creating a circular economy for electronics through innovative recycling processes, responsible refurbishment, and consumer education programs.

We partner with manufacturers, retailers, and consumers to extend product lifecycles, recover valuable materials, and ensure safe disposal of hazardous components.

Our goal is to reduce e-waste generation by 50% in the next decade through awareness campaigns and sustainable product design advocacy.
